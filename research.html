I am interested in the applications of Real Algebaric Geometry, Discrete Geometry and Optimization to science and engineering.

I have studied the theory of Sums of Nonnegative Circuit (SONC) Polynomials and their applications to the biochemical reaction networks during my Ph.D. research.

My M.Sc. project was on complex polynomials and hyperbolic geometry. Namely, I have studied the analogous results of Gauss-Lucas Theorem in Hyperbolic Geometry. You can ask for an electronic copy of my M.Sc. and Ph.D. work via e-mail.
